24-Hour watches

Jacopo Dondi

Jacopo Dondi was founded by partners Jake and Carmen, based in Perth, Australia, in 2016.

After initially thinking we had come up with a completely original concept, we soon discovered that not only was the concept already in existence, but that it had been around for almost 700 years, with Jacopo de’Dondi being commissioned to construct the world’s first clock face - a 24-hour face, of course - in Padua, Italy, in 1344.
