Seafood

With Lemon & Herbs

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Place the tomatoes, mozzarella, onion and basil in a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil to taste.
  3. Pour the dressing over the tomato mixture and toss.

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 cups tomatoes
  • 1 cup bocconcini mozzarella balls
  • 1/4 cup red onion
  • 1/3 cup small basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
